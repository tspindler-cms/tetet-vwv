The Douglas A-1H Skyraider (formerly designated AD-6) is a single-seat, piston-engined attack aircraft that became a legend for its ability to deliver massive ordnance loads with exceptional precision and endurance. Entering service just as the jet age began, the "Spad" was an anachronism that proved indispensable during the Vietnam War.
